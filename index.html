How it works

Statistics show that 70% of the success of therapy is about the relationship. Our unique match-making services considers more than just the qualifications of your therapist. We take into account their core-values, cultural background, identity, and religious beliefs to ensure you feel at home from the beginning.

Tell us what matters

Share your personality, values, and therapy goals through our thoughtful questionnaire. All responses are anonymous and confidential. We understand that being seen and understood by the right people is everything.

Our smart matching system considers your unique background, preferences, and needs to find therapists with the right expertise and cultural understanding.
