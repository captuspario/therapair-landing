üí° Common Questions

How do I update my listing?

Use our update form (link sent via email) or email contact@therapair.com.au with "Update My Listing" in the subject. Changes typically processed within 3-5 business days.

How do I remove my listing?

Email contact@therapair.com.au with "Remove My Listing" or use our removal form. Your listing will be removed from the live platform within 3-5 business days, with complete data deletion within 7 days.

Do I have to pay?

Currently, no. The demo phase is free for all participants. When we launch commercially (2026), we'll offer both free and paid tiers. You'll receive 60 days notice before any fees begin, with no obligation to upgrade.

Will I get client referrals?

We cannot guarantee specific numbers. Client inquiries depend on your specialization, location, our client traffic, and matching algorithm. Our goal is 3-5 quality inquiries per week for active practitioners, but this is a target, not a guarantee.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Therapair?

Therapair is a therapist-matching platform that helps you find mental health practitioners who truly understand your unique needs and identities. We go beyond location and specialty to match based on therapeutic approach, values, lived experience, and identity factors like LGBTQ+ affirming care, neurodiversity support, and cultural competency.

Is Therapair free to use?

Yes, currently. During our demo phase, both clients and therapists can use Therapair for free. When we launch commercially (2026), clients will always be able to use our matching service for free. Therapists will have both free and paid tier options ($99-179/month estimated for premium features).

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our algorithm considers 20+ factors beyond just location and specialty‚Äîincluding your identity (gender, sexuality, cultural background, neurodivergence), values, therapeutic approach preferences, communication style, session type (in-person vs. online), and urgency of need. We use AI to analyze these factors and recommend 2-3 therapists who best match your specific situation. The algorithm improves over time by learning from successful matches.

Are therapists verified?

We verify AHPRA registration for psychologists and check professional body membership (PACFA, ACA, APS) for counsellors and psychotherapists. However, we're a directory service, not a credentialing authority‚Äîwe encourage you to independently verify qualifications and registrations before beginning therapy.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your quiz responses are private and not shared with therapists unless you choose to contact them. You can complete the quiz anonymously (no email required). We comply with Australian Privacy Act 1988 and treat mental health information with heightened protection. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What makes Therapair different from Psychology Today?

Unlike Psychology Today (which gives you 200 therapists to manually search through), Therapair uses AI to match you with 2-3 specific recommendations based on your unique needs. We're built specifically for inclusive care (LGBTQ+, neurodivergent, culturally diverse communities), focused on Australia (AHPRA verified, NDIS aware), and use modern technology (mobile-first, fast, intuitive). We're the next generation of therapist matching.

How do I remove my information?

For therapists: Email contact@therapair.com.au with "Remove My Listing" or use our removal form. Listing removed within 3-5 business days, complete data deletion within 7 days.

For clients: Email us with "Delete My Data" to remove any quiz responses or contact information. Processed within 7 days.

See our Consent & Removal Policy for full details.
