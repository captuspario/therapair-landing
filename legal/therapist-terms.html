Eligibility & Professional Requirements

Who can be listed on Therapair:

Registered Psychologists

Current AHPRA registration, professional indemnity insurance

Accredited Counsellors

PACFA/ACA membership, relevant qualifications, insurance

Social Workers

AASW registration, mental health focus, insurance

Other Practitioners

Qualified modality, professional body membership (case-by-case)

Key Terms

Voluntary Participation

You choose whether to be listed. No obligation, no pressure. You can decline participation or remove your listing at any time without consequences.

Information Accuracy

You're responsible for ensuring your credentials, specializations, contact details, and practice information are accurate and current. Only claim qualifications you actually hold.

Professional Standards

You agree to maintain your professional obligations (AHPRA Code, PACFA/ACA Ethics), practice competently, and uphold inclusive practice commitments (if claimed).

No Guarantees

We cannot guarantee client referral volume, income, or practice outcomes. Therapair is a tool to help, not a guarantee of success.

Independent Practitioners

You're NOT our employee or contractor. You maintain full professional autonomy and are responsible for your own practice, taxes, and insurance.

Fees & Compensation

Current: FREE (Demo Phase)

No cost to be listed, no fees, no commission. Completely free during our demo/proof-of-concept phase.

Future: Paid Features (2026)

Estimated $99-179/month for premium features (analytics, priority placement, enhanced dashboard). You'll receive 60 days notice before any fees begin, with no obligation to upgrade.
