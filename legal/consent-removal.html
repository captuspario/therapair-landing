Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't respond to your email?

If we don't hear from you within 30 days, we assume you're not interested and won't list you. Your information will be deleted. No action required on your part.

Can I be listed now and remove later?

Yes, absolutely. Consent is always revocable. List for as long as it serves you, remove whenever you want.

How do I remove just my photo but keep my listing?

Email us with "Remove Photo Only" and we'll delete your photo but keep your text listing active.

Can I change my mind after removal?

Yes! Withdrawal is not permanent. If you change your mind, you're welcome back anytime. Just use the participation form again or email us. No penalties, no grudges.
